Have you watched this show? Not yet? Let's start this weekend. The storyline is rich and the emotion displayed by the actors is solid. You can feel the emotion to the heart. This is one of the must watch series in my opinion.

Parks and Recreation

  • Rating: 9.5/10
  • Genre: Comedy, Drama

One of the funniest show I have ever watched. The actors really got into the characters.

Raising Hope

  • Rating: 9.6/10

This is another one of Greg Garcia masterpiece besides My Name is Earl. I really love this series. It is so funny and the story is unique. Unfortunately it was discontinued after four seasons.
